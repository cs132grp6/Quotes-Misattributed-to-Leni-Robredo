Maki-BeKI. 'Wag mata-QUOTE!

In Japanese, the word beki (べき) translates to “should”, obligating an action based on moral and sound judgment (Pitorri, 2021).

This project, entitled Twitter Trend Analysis of Statements Misattributed to Leni Robredo During the 2022 Philippine Election Period, aims to study Twitter engagements related to misquotes attributed to the former Vice President. It attempts to understand how intentionally deceptive information is propagated through social media, and aims to help curtail disinformation through Data Science.

Because fighting disinformation is a should.
