CONCLUSIONS

Conclusion

From the data trend, it can be concluded that the number of tweets with quotes misattributed to Robredo, and the number of likes increased as the 2022 election came closer.


Implications

Based on the gathered data and supporting information, it can further be generalized that:


  1. The tweeted misquotes and misinformation were intentional and orchestrated. This was evident in the lack of recent trigger. The misattributed statements propagated in tweets were not related to recent events, and were resurrected from old misquotes, used one at a time.

  2. Misinformation/disinformation was intended to malign Robredo, because she was the strongest rival of Marcos Jr for presidency. Moreover, the disinformation tweets also serve the purpose of diverting attention from events disadvantageous to Marcos Jr.

This study somehow proves that disinformation in Twitter was utilized as a propaganda tool in the 2022 Philippine national elections, and possibly, beyond. Due to its potential ramifications on our political, economic, and social landscape, it becomes our responsibility as Filipinos to be aware of and act against disinformation in any form.
